## 🎨 Design Specifications

### Colors
- **Primary**: #0891B2 (Cyan - Trust & Medical)
- **Secondary**: #06B6D4 (Teal - Accents)
- **Tertiary**: #10B981 (Green - Wellness)
- **Neutrals**: Slate grays for text & backgrounds
- **Surfaces**: White with 70% opacity for glassmorphism

### Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts, variable weight)
- **Display**: 48-70px, weight 700
- **Heading**: 24-32px, weight 600-700
- **Body**: 16-18px, weight 400
- **Label**: 12-14px, weight 500-600

### Effects
- **Glassmorphism**: blur(12px) backdrop filter
- **Shadows**: Soft shadows with color-specific opacity
- **Gradients**: Soft, multi-stop gradients
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions (300-500ms)
- **Hover States**: Scale, translate, shadow, opacity changes
